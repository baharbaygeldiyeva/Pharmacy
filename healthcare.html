ABOUT US

Abaris Healthcare is an innovative and solution oriented healthcare company with a focus on manufacturing and marketing small and large volume parenteral required by healthcare professionals to deliver quality services to patients. Our manufacturing facility is located in Mehsana, Gujarat and it operates PAN India. We take pride in our distribution network and exports that spans beyond 20 countries. We are a reputable manufacturer of healthcare products. We have the technical know how and industrial equipment to achieve clear cut designs, maintain standard manufacturing processes and quality control, and ensure quality product delivery. Our team explore new approaches and chart a new path in healthcare by offering customized pharmaceutical formulations and sterile parenteral formulations to meet increasing demands. We are fully committed to provide quality driven care for the masses and ensure they have access to the right healthcare services they need. We continue to invest hugely in research and product development to support the advancement of the medical sector. Business Overview

Distribution

Our products are for global distribution. We have an effective distribution network. We have field staff in different cities in India that promote our products through channel sales (Distributors). Health facilities, healthcare professionals, and patients find it easy to access our products through our distribution network

Contract Manufacturing:

Our operations also cover contract manufacturing. We customize formulations according to specific requirements as a specialty drug manufacturer. We also do private labels for top MNCs

Exports:

We export our products to over 20 countries. Our products are exported to regions such as Africa, CIS, LATAM, and South East Asia. We like to share the richness of our products across national lines thereby making it possible for more people to have access to bespoke products for their needs.
